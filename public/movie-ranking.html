The Best Movies According to Dave.

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Demon Slayer

This is my favourite anime . I love the beautiful images
as well as the protagonist who was recognized as
Tanjiro Kamado in the story.

The 100

Really cool sci-fi movie, kinda gives me a strong urge
to continue learning more about technology.

Lucifer

Super beautiful film. Really artistic how the protagonist
played the role so well that i start to imagine that there is
no one who will be able to play the role better coupled
with the twists and suspence. I highly recommend this movie.
